About Swapnobaj-69

Learn about our journey, mission, and commitment to quality fisheries.

Our Story

Swapnobaj-69 was founded in 2020 with a vision to revolutionize the fisheries industry in Bangladesh. What started as a small family business has now grown into a trusted name in both regular food fish and ornamental aquarium fish supply.

Our founder, with over 15 years of experience in fisheries, recognized the need for a reliable source of high-quality fish that adheres to sustainable farming practices. This led to the establishment of Swapnobaj-69, which combines traditional knowledge with modern techniques to bring the best to your table and aquariums.

Today, we operate several fish farms across Bangladesh, employ over 50 dedicated staff, and serve thousands of satisfied customers each month. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction remains at the heart of everything we do.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, sustainably farmed fish and aquarium products that meet the diverse needs of our customers while promoting responsible aquaculture practices.

Our Vision

To become the leading fisheries company in Bangladesh, recognized for excellence in quality, innovation, and sustainable practices, while making a positive impact on local communities and the environment.
